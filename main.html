Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto is a beautiful holiday destination known for its peaceful temples, traditional tea houses, colorful gardens, and historic streets. It is a perfect place to enjoy culture, nature, and delicious food.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine
  2. Kinkaku-ji Golden Pavilion
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  4. Gion District
  5. Kiyomizu-dera Temple

Must-Try Foods

  • Matcha sweets
  • Yudofu tofu
  • Kaiseki meal
  • Nishin soba
  • Yatsuhashi sweets
