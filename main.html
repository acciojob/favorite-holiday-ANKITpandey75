Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is one of the world's most popular travel destinations. Known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, art museums, and delicious cuisine, Paris offers a unique blend of culture and romance.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to thousands of famous artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - One of the most famous avenues in the world.
  5. Montmartre - A charming district known for its artistic heritage.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant - A buttery and flaky pastry.
  • Macarons - Colorful and delicious French cookies.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Baguette - A classic French bread.
  • Ratatouille - A traditional vegetable dish.
