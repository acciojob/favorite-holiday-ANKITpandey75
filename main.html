Bengaluru


Bengaluru, also known as the Silicon Valley of India, is a vibrant city famous for its pleasant weather, technology hubs, beautiful parks, and rich cultural heritage.

  1. Lalbagh Botanical Garden - A famous garden with diverse plant species.
  2. Cubbon Park - A large green space in the heart of the city.
  3. Bangalore Palace - A historic royal palace.
  4. Vidhana Soudha - An iconic government building.
  5. ISKCON Temple - A renowned spiritual and cultural center.
