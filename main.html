Goa


Goa is one of India's most popular holiday destinations, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood.

  1. Baga Beach - Famous for water sports and nightlife.
  2. Calangute Beach - Known as the "Queen of Beaches".
  3. Fort Aguada - A historic Portuguese fort.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus - A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  5. Dudhsagar Falls - A stunning four-tiered waterfall.
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Vindaloo
