Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto is a peaceful holiday destination known for ancient temples, quiet gardens, bamboo forests, traditional tea houses, and beautiful seasonal scenery.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine with its famous red torii gates.
  2. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove for a calm forest walk.
  3. Kinkaku-ji, the stunning Golden Pavilion.
  4. Kiyomizu-dera Temple with beautiful city views.
  5. Gion District, known for traditional streets and culture.

Must-Try Foods

  • Matcha sweets made with Kyoto green tea.
  • Yudofu, a warm tofu dish served near temples.
  • Kaiseki, a traditional multi-course meal.
  • Ramen from local Kyoto noodle shops.
  • Mochi with sweet red bean filling.
